About Me

I have both academic and job experience using .NET, C#, and SQL. I have written code using Java and C#, and have developed applications ranging from web page development to reports that query databases. My primary technical interest is to write and support original code in applications that solve customer/client problems. Positions that support this goal or help me achieve it are of interest to me. Further, I am passionate about delivering high quality work products to my employer to contribute to the success of the company.

Since graduating with a B.S. degree Computer Science from UH-Victoria in 2015, I have worked as a junior software developer at Stupp Corporation, an oil services company that manufactures drilling pipes, and Huntington-Ingalls, one of the premier military ship builders in the world. Most recently, I have enrolled in the UT-Austin Center for Professional Education- Houston Coding Boot Camp. Upon completion in February 2019, I will have in-depth knowledge of a variety of coding languages and platforms, including those that support the activities of a full stack web developer.
